A lion

The lion is a large cat of the genus Panthera native to Africa and India. It has a muscular, broad-chested body, short, rounded head, round ears, and a hairy tuft at the end of its tail. It is sexually dimorphic; adult male lions are larger than females and have a prominent mane.

  • Scientific Name: Scientific name: Panthera leo
  • Average Length: 1.8 – 2.1 m (Male, Head and body), 1.6 – 1.8 m (Female, Head and body) Mass: 190 kg (Male, Adult), 130 kg (Female, Adult)
  • Average Lifespan: 15 – 16 years (Female, Adult, In the wild), 8 – 10 years (Male, Adult, In the wild)
  • Height: 1.2 m (Male, Adult, At Shoulder), 90 – 110 cm (Female, Adult, At Shoulder) Speed: 80 km/h (Maximum, In Short Bursts)
